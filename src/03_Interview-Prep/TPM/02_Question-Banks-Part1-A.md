**Complete question bank organized by difficulty level** for Technical Project Manager interview preparation.

### Agile & Scrum Basics

1. What is the difference between Agile and Scrum?
2. What is a sprint?
3. What happens in daily stand-up?
4. What is a Product Backlog vs Sprint Backlog?
5. What is the role of a Scrum Master?
6. What is a user story? What format do you use?
7. What is acceptance criteria?
8. What is Definition of Done (DoD)?
9. What is a retrospective?
10. What is velocity?

### Project Management Basics

11. What tools have you used for project tracking? (JIRA, Azure DevOps, Trello)
12. What is a Gantt chart?
13. How do you track progress in a sprint?
14. What is risk vs issue?
15. What is a dependency?
16. What is scope creep?
17. What is the difference between estimation in hours vs story points?

### Technical Basics

18. What programming languages have you worked with?
19. What is an API?
20. What is continuous integration?

## ðŸŸ¨ MEDIUM LEVEL (Role-Critical â€” 25 Questions)

### Technical Delivery

1. Explain the SDLC phases in detail.
2. How do you write good user stories?
3. How do you perform story estimation?
4. How do you calculate sprint capacity?
5. How do you create and manage a sprint board?
6. How do you track developer productivity?
7. How do you handle a delayed story in mid-sprint?
8. How do you ensure quality in code delivery?
9. What is your experience with code reviews?
10. What is your CI/CD pipeline setup?

### Architecture Concepts

11. What is the difference between monolithic and microservices architecture?
12. What is a REST API?
13. What is an API gateway?
14. What is caching, and why is it used?
15. Explain synchronous vs asynchronous communication.

### Leadership & Stakeholders

16. How do you resolve conflicts between team members?
17. How do you handle disagreements about technical design?
18. How do you manage stakeholder expectations?
19. How do you communicate risks to leadership?
20. How do you lead cross-functional teams?

### Agile Practical Scenarios

21. Your developer says task estimates are unrealistic. What do you do?
22. Product Owner keeps changing requirements. How do you handle?
23. QA team is overloaded. What is your plan?
24. A senior engineer is not cooperating. What's your approach?
25. You have a hard deadline with limited resources. What do you do?

## ðŸŸ¥ ADVANCED LEVEL (Deep Technical + Delivery â€” 25 Questions)

### Architecture & Technical Depth

1. Design a high-level architecture for an e-commerce system.
2. Explain circuit breaker pattern.
3. What is event-driven architecture?
4. Explain microservices communication patterns (REST, gRPC, message queues).
5. How do you design a fault-tolerant system?
6. How do you handle database transactions in microservices?
7. Explain Saga pattern.
8. What is CQRS?
9. What is API versioning?
10. How do you perform load testing?

### DevOps + SDLC

11. Explain your DevOps pipeline end-to-end.
12. What branching strategies have you used? (GitFlow, trunk-based)
13. How do you automate deployments?
14. What is blue-green deployment?
15. What is canary release?

### Advanced Agile & Planning

16. How do you manage multiple teams working on a single project?
17. What Agile metrics do you track besides velocity?
18. Explain cycle time vs lead time.
19. How do you handle inter-team dependencies?
20. How do you plan for long-term releases (PI Planning)?

### Critical Scenarios

21. The production environment is down. How do you respond?
22. Backend and frontend teams blame each other. What do you do?
23. Your team consistently misses sprint commitments. What's your fix?
24. A developer is technically good but has poor communication. How do you help them?
25. You discover major technical debt late in the project. How do you handle it?
