### Strategic & Leadership Capability

1. What is the biggest technical project you have led end-to-end?
2. How do you make architectural decisions when the team is divided?
3. How do you balance business deadlines with technical complexity?
4. Describe a major project failure you handled.
5. How do you scale engineering teams from 5 to 50 members?
6. What frameworks do you use to manage complex programs (SAFe, LeSS, DAD)?
7. How do you measure engineering performance at the team level?
8. How do you prioritize tech debt vs new features?
9. How do you manage conflicts between architect and product manager?
10. How do you mentor senior developers and leads?

### Risk & Delivery Ownership

11. Describe a risk that became a production issue. How did you fix it?
12. How do you handle a project that is 2 months behind schedule?
13. How do you rescue a failing project?
14. How do you perform capacity planning for large teams?
15. How do you justify additional headcount to leadership?

### Advanced Technical Delivery

16. How do you coordinate architecture across microservices?
17. When do you consider rewriting vs refactoring a system?
18. How do you handle performance issues across distributed systems?
19. What is your strategy for observability?
20. How do you ensure release stability?

### High-Stakes Scenarios

21. You have a go-live in 4 days, but QA found blocking bugs. What do you do?
22. Product Owner demands an impossible deadline. How do you negotiate?
23. A senior engineer quits suddenly. How do you mitigate impact?
24. The architecture approved earlier is no longer suitable. What now?
25. A CEO asks for a roadmap update in 5 minutes. What do you present?
