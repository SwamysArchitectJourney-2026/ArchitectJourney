# Real-World Agile & Scrum Scenarios

**Practical scenarios and answer frameworks** for Agile/Scrum expertise round.

---

## Scenario 1: Sprint is Failing Halfway

**Answer Framework:**

* Re-assess backlog
* Re-prioritize with Product Owner
* Remove low-priority stories
* Protect the team
* Communicate transparently to stakeholders

---

## Scenario 2: Developers Argue Over Design

**Use:**

* Fact-based decision making
* Architecture review
* RFC document
* Time-box the discussion
* Final decision by Architect

---

## Scenario 3: Requirements Keep Changing

**Use:**

* Change management
* Update backlog
* Re-estimate
* Communicate impact
* Negotiate timeline

---

## Scenario 4: Stakeholders Push Unrealistic Deadlines

**Use:**

* Data-driven communication
* Past velocity
* Resource capacity
* Risk implications
* Offer options (scope reduction, staggered release, phased delivery)
