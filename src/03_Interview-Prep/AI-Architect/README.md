## üìù Interview Tips

### Before the Interview
1. Review the **Cheat Sheet** (`./06_Cheat-Sheet.md`)
2. Practice drawing ASCII diagrams
3. Memorize the 30-second architecture summary
4. Review the five must-know talking points

### During the Interview
1. Ask clarifying questions before whiteboarding
2. Explain your reasoning and trade-offs
3. Mention scalability, security, and observability
4. Show architect-level thinking

### Whiteboard Tips
- Draw clean, simple boxes (7 max)
- Explain as you draw
- Mention key Azure services
- Discuss trade-offs
