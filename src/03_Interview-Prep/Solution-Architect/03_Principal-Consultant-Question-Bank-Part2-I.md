## ðŸ“‹ Evaluation Criteria

### Architecture Depth

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Deep technical knowledge
* Understanding of tradeoffs
* Real-world experience
* Ability to design at scale
* Knowledge of best practices

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Reference specific technologies
* Explain design decisions
* Discuss scalability considerations
* Mention real-world constraints
* Show knowledge of patterns

---

### Clarity

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Clear communication
* Structured thinking
* Logical flow
* Easy to follow
* Concise explanations

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Use frameworks (requirements â†’ architecture â†’ components)
* Start with high-level, then dive deep
* Use diagrams when helpful
* Summarize key points
* Ask clarifying questions

---

### Consulting Communication

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Client-facing skills
* Business alignment
* Stakeholder management
* Presentation skills
* Professional demeanor

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Connect technical solutions to business value
* Discuss cost considerations
* Address stakeholder concerns
* Present confidently
* Show empathy for client needs

---

### Tradeoff Explanation

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Decision-making process
* Reasoning ability
* Understanding of alternatives
* Risk assessment
* Balanced perspective

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Discuss multiple options
* Explain pros and cons
* Justify your recommendation
* Acknowledge limitations
* Show nuanced thinking

---

### Senior-Level Thinking

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Strategic perspective
* Long-term vision
* Platform thinking
* Governance awareness
* Leadership qualities

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Think beyond immediate problem
* Consider scalability (3-5 years)
* Discuss governance and compliance
* Show platform mindset
* Demonstrate influence skills
