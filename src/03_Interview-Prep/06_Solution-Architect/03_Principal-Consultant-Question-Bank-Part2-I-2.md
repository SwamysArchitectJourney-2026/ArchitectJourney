### Tradeoff Explanation

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Decision-making process
* Reasoning ability
* Understanding of alternatives
* Risk assessment
* Balanced perspective

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Discuss multiple options
* Explain pros and cons
* Justify your recommendation
* Acknowledge limitations
* Show nuanced thinking

---

### Senior-Level Thinking

**What Interviewers Look For:**

* Strategic perspective
* Long-term vision
* Platform thinking
* Governance awareness
* Leadership qualities

**How to Demonstrate:**

* Think beyond immediate problem
* Consider scalability (3-5 years)
* Discuss governance and compliance
* Show platform mindset
* Demonstrate influence skills
